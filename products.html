Crate-Size Corrugated Boxes

Taking a strong environmental and sustainable initiative, we have launched heavy-duty crate-sized corrugated boxes as a direct replacement for traditional plastic crates. These boxes offer the same structural integrity and volume capacity, while being 100% recyclable, biodegradable, and significantly reducing carbon footprint.
