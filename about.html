About Us

Established in 2008

D.A.V. Containers Pvt. Ltd., situated in Ladwa, Kurukshetra, Haryana, is a well-established and trusted name in the packaging industry. With over a decade of experience, we have cemented our position as a leading manufacturer of premium packaging solutions.

Our state-of-the-art facility features an advanced semi-automatic manufacturing unit, enabling us to deliver high volumes of corrugated boxes with unparalleled precision and durability.

Our Mission

To provide robust, reliable, and aesthetically pleasing packaging that not only protects the contents during transit but also serves as a powerful tool for brand awareness. We believe that a high-quality printed box is the first step in customer retention.
